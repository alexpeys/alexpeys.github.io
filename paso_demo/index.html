CONTINGENCY PLANS

Weather Contingencies

If Rainy:

  • Replace morning walks with shopping at Paso Market Walk (1803 Spring Street), which has several indoor/covered areas where dogs may be welcome (confirm individual store policies).
  • Focus on wineries with indoor seating that allows dogs.
  • Consider visiting Paso Robles Children's Museum or Pioneer Museum if traveling with children (check dog policies).

If Unusually Hot (85°F+):

  • Schedule outdoor activities for early morning or evening.
  • Ensure your dog has plenty of water and shade.
  • Consider adding a midday break at your accommodation to rest.
  • Check that your dog's paws aren't burning on hot pavement.
