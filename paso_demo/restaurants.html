Dog-Friendly Restaurants

Paso Robles Dining Options

Home (Itinerary) | Travel Plan | Accommodations | Dining | Wineries | Activities | Reservations

Most restaurants require dogs to be leashed and well-behaved on outdoor patios. It's recommended to call ahead if you're bringing your dog, especially for dinner reservations. Water bowls are often provided, but bringing your own is appreciated.

Breakfast Options

Springside Cafe

  • Description: Family restaurant with a fenced patio that welcomes dogs.
  • Price Range: $$
  • Notable Menu Items: Omelets, blueberry pancakes, French toast.
  • Hours: Daily 7:00am - 2:00pm.
  • Reservations: Walk-ins welcome.
  • Contact: (805) 238-2629
  • Address: 1646 Spring Street, Paso Robles
  • Website: springside-restaurant.com

Spearhead Coffee

  • Description: Specialty coffee shop with outdoor seating perfect for people-watching with your dog.
  • Price Range: $
  • Notable Menu Items: Ethically sourced coffees, espresso, macchiato, cappuccino, chai.
  • Hours: Monday-Friday 6:30am-6:00pm, Saturday-Sunday 7:00am-6:00pm.
  • Reservations: Not required.
  • Contact: (805) 296-3328
  • Address: 619 12th Street, Paso Robles
  • Website: spearheadcoffee.com

TASTE! Craft Eatery

  • Description: Family-owned eatery with a dog-friendly back patio.
  • Price Range: $$
  • Notable Menu Items: Weekend brunch menu (served until 3pm).
  • Hours: Daily from 11am, weekend brunch available.
  • Reservations: Available through Tock.
  • Contact: (805) 226-8000
  • Address: 810 11th Street, Paso Robles
  • Website: tastepasorobles.com

Los Robles Cafe

  • Description: Casual Mexican eatery with sidewalk tables for dogs.
  • Price Range: $
  • Notable Menu Items: Mexican breakfast items, tacos, burritos, enchiladas.
  • Hours: Breakfast and lunch hours.
  • Reservations: Walk-ins welcome.
  • Contact: (805) 238-3770
  • Address: 1420 Spring Street, Paso Robles

Thomas Hill Organics
